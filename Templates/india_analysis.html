      Forest Cover

      How can we preserve Forests

    • More trees should be planted to increase the forest cover.
    • Trees should be selected according to the geographical conditions of a particular region and proper care should be taken during the growth of trees.
    • Prevention of exploitation of forestry and forest products is necessary for the conservation of forest.
    • The existing forests should be protected from diseases by spraying chemicals, antibiotics or development of pest-resistant strains of trees.
    • Natural forest fires are also responsible for the destruction of huge forest covers. Latest fire fighting techniques should be adopted to conserve the forest.

      How can we improve WQI

    • Waste water management
    • Clean up and restore existing waterways and water bodies
    • Reduce water footprint
    • Efficient irrigation
    • Rain water harvesting
